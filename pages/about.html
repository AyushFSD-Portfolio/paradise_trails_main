Our Story

Paradise Trails Sri Lanka is a nature-focused hiking and adventure group based in Ja-Ela, Sri Lanka. Their mission is to foster a deep connection with nature through immersive outdoor experiences. They organize guided hikes, camping trips, and eco-adventures across the island, promoting sustainable travel and environmental appreciation
